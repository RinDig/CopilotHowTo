Microsoft 365 & Copilot Setup Guide

A step-by-step guide to setting up a demo environment for Pacific Life engagements.

Licensing Options

For Most Users: Business Plans

Ideal for individuals and small teams who need a robust demo environment without the complexity of enterprise-level administration.

  • Microsoft 365 Business Standard
  • Microsoft 365 Business Premium
Go to Business Setup

For Advanced Needs: Enterprise Plans

For situations requiring more advanced features, compliance, and security controls, or for larger-scale demos.

  • Microsoft 365 E3
  • Microsoft 365 E5
Go to Enterprise Setup

Step-by-Step: Setting Up a Business Account

1Purchase a Microsoft 365 Business Plan

Navigate to the Microsoft 365 for Business pricing page and choose either Business Standard or Business Premium.

2Create a New Business Account

Follow the prompts to set up a new account. You'll create a new user (e.g., `yourname@yourcompany.onmicrosoft.com`) and provide payment details.

3Access the Microsoft 365 Admin Center

Once your purchase is complete, go to admin.microsoft.com and sign in with your new business credentials.

Step-by-Step: Setting Up an Enterprise Account

1Purchase a Microsoft 365 Enterprise Plan

Go to the Microsoft 365 Enterprise pricing page and select a plan (E3 or E5 are recommended).

2Follow the Enterprise Setup Process

The process is similar to the business setup but may involve more detailed configuration options. You'll create a new tenant and a global administrator account.

3Access the Microsoft 365 Admin Center

As with the business setup, you'll manage your licenses and users through the admin.microsoft.com portal.

Preparing Your Laptop

1Create a Clean Work Profile

In your web browser (Edge is recommended), create a new profile and sign in with your new Microsoft 365 work account. This keeps your demo environment separate from your personal accounts.

2Sign Into Microsoft 365 Apps

Open each Microsoft 365 application (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams) and sign out of any personal accounts. Then, sign in with your new work account. You may need to click 'update liscense' for Copilot button to show up in EACH of the office apps.

3Set Up OneDrive

Set up OneDrive to sync with your new work account. This will allow you to easily save and access demo files.

Data Hygiene and Best Practices

Use Only Synthetic Data

Never use real client data or personally identifiable information in your demo environment. Create mock documents, spreadsheets, and presentations.

Keep Profiles Separate

Always use a dedicated browser profile for your demo work to avoid any crossover with personal or other work accounts.

Do Not Connect to Client Tenants

This demo environment should remain completely independent and should not be connected to any Pacific Life or other client systems.

Final Checklist

This guide is intended for internal use only. Please do not share with external parties.
